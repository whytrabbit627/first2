## What Ovia Is

Ovia is a health app company with a suite of reproductive health products — fertility tracking, pregnancy tracking, and parenting. The Ovia Pregnancy app is the middle product in that suite, designed to accompany you from positive test to birth.

It sits at the more serious, data-oriented end of the pregnancy tracking app market. Where some apps lean heavily on cute baby-development animations (your baby is the size of a kumquat!), Ovia leads with health information, symptom tracking, and personalised insights.

## Core Features

**Weekly development updates.** Each week of pregnancy, Ovia sends a personalised update on what's happening developmentally for your baby and what changes to expect in your body. The content is medically reviewed and is pitched at a level of detail that treats you as an adult who can handle real information.

**Symptom logging.** Log nausea, fatigue, mood, spotting, and dozens of other symptoms. Ovia tracks these over time and flags patterns worth discussing with your provider. It won't replace your midwife, but having a symptom diary available at appointments is genuinely useful.

**Kick counter.** From around 28 weeks, your provider will likely recommend counting fetal movements. Ovia's kick counter tracks your sessions and lets you see patterns in your baby's movement. Noticing a change in movement pattern early is one of the most important things you can do for late pregnancy safety.

**Appointment reminders.** Enter your appointment schedule and Ovia reminds you in advance. Simple but useful in a period when brain fog is real.

**Weight and blood pressure tracking.** For those who want to monitor these alongside appointments rather than only in clinic.

## What Makes It Different from Other Apps

The pregnancy app market is crowded. What distinguishes Ovia:

**Less noise.** The interface is cleaner and less infantilising than several competitors. It doesn't feel like it was designed for someone who needs their information wrapped in cartoon characters.

**Health record integration.** In some regions, Ovia integrates with electronic health records, allowing it to import data from your provider. This varies by country and healthcare system.

**Privacy considerations.** Ovia has been the subject of coverage regarding their data practices — specifically, that the app was offered to employees through employer health programmes and that de-identified data was shared. If data privacy is a priority for you, read their current privacy policy before creating an account. They've updated their practices since the most prominent coverage, but it's worth being informed.

## The Free vs. Premium Version

The free version of Ovia covers the core tracking features. The premium version adds additional content and some personalisation features. The free version is sufficient for most users — evaluate whether the premium additions are worth the cost for you after using the free version for a few weeks.

## Sharing with a Partner

Ovia allows you to connect a partner account that gives a secondary view of your pregnancy data. Some partners find this a useful way to stay connected and informed; others find the information more useful experienced together rather than separately. Worth knowing it's an option.

## Bottom Line

Ovia is a solid, well-designed pregnancy tracking app that respects your intelligence and delivers genuinely useful health information. If you want a tracker that goes deeper than baby-vegetable comparisons, it's worth trying. Be aware of the data privacy considerations and make an informed decision about what you share.
