## What the Boppy Does

The Boppy Original is a firm, C-shaped pillow designed to wrap around your waist and create a stable platform that brings baby up to the right height for feeding. Whether you're breastfeeding or bottle-feeding, it solves the same mechanical problem: a newborn is heavy enough that holding them at feeding height for 20–40 minutes, multiple times a day, is genuinely tiring — on your arms, your shoulders, and your lower back.

With the Boppy in place, you can relax your arms and let the pillow bear the weight while you focus on the feed itself. For breastfeeding parents establishing a latch in the early days, removing the physical strain of positioning is genuinely useful.

## Beyond Feeding

The Boppy has a second life as a development prop. From around two months, it's excellent for supervised tummy time — propped over the Boppy, babies get a gentler introduction to the position that helps build neck and shoulder strength. Later, it supports babies learning to sit, used as a soft bumper on either side.

Most families find it earns its space through at least the first six months purely as a feeding tool, then gets additional use as a tummy time prop. By the time baby is reliably sitting independently, the Boppy tends to migrate to the back of a cupboard — but you'll have had considerable use from it by then.

## Nursing vs. Bottle Feeding

For breastfeeding parents, the C-shaped design works in the cradle hold position most commonly taught in the early days. Different holds — the football hold, for example — may not use the Boppy as naturally. Experiment in the early days rather than assuming you need to use it a specific way.

For bottle-feeding parents and partners doing overnight feeds, the Boppy is equally useful. Getting baby to a comfortable height reduces strain regardless of how they're being fed.

## Cover and Washing

The Boppy comes with a slipcover that's machine washable — useful given the frequency of spit-up in the newborn phase. Additional covers are sold separately and give you something to use while the original is in the wash. The pillow insert itself should be spot-cleaned rather than machine washed.

## One Consideration

The Boppy is sized for adults. Very petite parents sometimes find that the C-shape sits awkwardly around a smaller waist. The My Brest Friend pillow (a common alternative) has an adjustable strap and a flatter, more stable surface that some parents prefer. Both are widely recommended — it comes down to body shape and personal preference.

## Bottom Line

The Boppy is one of those baby products that's been around long enough to have become a genuine staple for good reason. It makes feeds more comfortable, gives you one less thing to struggle with in the newborn phase, and transitions into a useful development tool. It's a reliable registry addition that almost always gets used.
