## Why Car Seat Installation Matters More Than Brand

A car seat only protects a child if it's installed correctly. Studies consistently show that a significant proportion of car seats in everyday use are installed incorrectly — sometimes severely so. This is why ease of correct installation is one of the most important factors in choosing an infant seat, not just safety ratings in lab conditions.

The Chicco KeyFit 35 has earned its reputation precisely here: it installs correctly more reliably than most competitors. The combination of a level indicator, clear LATCH connectors, and a well-designed base makes it difficult to get wrong.

## What the KeyFit 35 Offers

**LATCH installation:** The LATCH (Lower Anchors and Tethers for CHildren) system uses built-in anchors in your car to secure the base without relying on the seatbelt. The KeyFit 35's LATCH connectors are easy to attach and give an audible click when properly secured.

**Built-in level indicator:** A bubble level on the base tells you immediately whether the seat is at the correct recline angle for infant rear-facing use. No guessing required.

**Spring-loaded levelling foot:** Adjusts to match the slope of your car's rear seat, maintaining the correct recline on uneven seat surfaces. Many competing seats require shimming with pool noodles or rolled towels to achieve the same result.

**Infant head insert:** Newborns need additional head and body support to sit correctly in the seat. The KeyFit 35 comes with a padded insert that fits appropriately for small newborns and is removed as baby grows.

## Weight and Height Limits

The KeyFit 35 accommodates rear-facing infants from 1.8kg (4lb) to 15.9kg (35lb), with a height limit of 76cm (30 inches). This covers the full range of newborn to the typical age at which parents transition to a convertible seat — usually somewhere between 9 and 18 months depending on the child's growth rate.

## Travel System Compatibility

The KeyFit 35 is compatible with a wide range of strollers via adapters, including most UPPAbaby, Bugaboo, and Chicco models. For families planning a travel system (car seat that clicks directly onto the stroller without disturbing a sleeping baby), check compatibility for your specific stroller model.

## What It Doesn't Have

The KeyFit 35 is not a luxury seat. The padding and fabric are functional rather than premium. It lacks some features found on higher-priced seats, such as a load leg (which provides additional crash protection by bracing against the car floor). For families prioritising advanced safety features over budget, seats like the Cybex Cloud Q or Nuna PIPA Rx include additional engineering — at a meaningfully higher price.

## Having Your Installation Checked

Regardless of which seat you choose, it's worth having the installation inspected by a certified Child Passenger Safety Technician (CPST) before baby arrives. Many fire stations and paediatric hospitals offer free checks. Even a correctly installed seat benefits from a second set of expert eyes.

## Bottom Line

The Chicco KeyFit 35 is the right choice for most families: consistently top safety ratings, easy correct installation, broad compatibility, and a price point that doesn't require the cost to be the main deciding factor. Install it before your due date, have it checked, and focus on the rest of your preparation.
